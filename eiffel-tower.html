La Tour Eiffel

Paris, France

Constructed 1887-1889

Click here to see the construction of The Eiffel Tower!

The construction schedule

  1. The first floor was completed on the 1st April 1888
  2. The second floor was completed on the 14 August 1888
  3. The top section was completed on the 31st March 1889

Facts and figures

  • The tower has 1665 steps
  • 2.5 million rivets were used
  • 7,300 tonnes or iron were used in construction
  • 60 tonnes of paint were used
